Date: 2022-10-16

Welcome the 20th CPC Congress and Promote High-Quality Development

On the morning of October 16, the مجموعة KIWL Party Branch organized Party members and cadres to watch the opening ceremony of the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China. Party members and cadres were greatly inspired by General Secretary Xi Jinping's report, especially the great transformations of the new era over the past decade.

Quality is the cornerstone of core product competitiveness. For many years, مجموعة KIWL has upheld the corporate values of "Innovation, Craftsmanship, Dedication, and Harmony," driven by technological innovation. On the journey into the new era, the company is building a new development pattern for the fluid-power industry and promoting high-quality development. In September, KIWL welcomed second-party audits from two major OEM customers, XCMG Construction Machinery and Weichai Lovol. Expert teams conducted a comprehensive and systematic review of KIWL's quality management system.

Seizing this opportunity, مجموعة KIWL held a management improvement meeting themed "Welcome the 20th CPC Congress, Forge Ahead on a New Journey, and Promote KIWL High-Quality Development." The company committed to deepening management comprehensively, pursuing a "zero-defect" goal, staying firmly on the path of high-quality development, and continuously improving innovation in key core components to contribute KIWL's "نواة الصين" to China's high-end equipment manufacturing industry.

Riding the momentum of the 20th CPC Congress, مجموعة KIWL will continue its innovation-driven development strategy and remain dedicated to building a نواة الصين for national heavy equipment with KIWL wisdom, solutions, and strength.
