Stay Firm in Direction and Forge Ahead on the New Era Journey
Date: 2021-11-30

KIWL Group Party Branch

Special Study Session on the Sixth Plenary Session of the 19th CPC Central Committee

On November 30, the KIWL Group Party Branch held a special study session on the spirit of the Sixth Plenary Session of the 19th CPC Central Committee. Joint Party Branch Secretary and Chairman Jiang Wei led the study. All Party members, cadres, probationary members, and activists participated, with branch venues in Shanghai and Xuzhou joining online.

The meeting pointed out

The Sixth Plenary Session was held at a major historical juncture as the Party celebrated its centenary and advanced toward the second centenary goal of building a modern socialist country.

The meeting emphasized

Studying and implementing the Session's spirit is a major political task. All cadres must study deeply, grasp the core meaning, unify thought and action, and translate new concepts into concrete measures for high-quality development.

The meeting stressed

First, raise political awareness. Party members should prioritize studying the Session's spirit. Leading cadres should set an example.

Second, plan carefully. Departments should coordinate under the Party Branch and ensure full learning coverage.

Third, strengthen supervision. The Party Branch will track implementation across all units and resolve issues promptly.
