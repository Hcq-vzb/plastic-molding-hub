Date: 2021-05-19

Electric Injection Molding Machine Trends at CHINAPLAS 2026

CHINAPLAS 2026 showcases how electric injection molding machines dominate efficiency-focused purchasing. Brand owners and contract molders seek lower kWh per part, quieter plants, and data-ready controllers.

All-Electric vs. Hybrid Electrics

Full electric presses eliminate hydraulic oil cooling and leak risks. Hybrid models pair electric screw drive with servo-hydraulic clamp for thick-wall packaging — evaluate total kWh on your actual shot weight and cycle.

Smart Monitoring and MES Hooks

OEM controllers now export OPC-UA and MQTT for scrap, cycle time, and energy per mold. Integrate with shop-floor dashboards to benchmark night-shift performance.

Applications in Packaging

Thin-wall containers, caps, and medical disposables benefit from precise velocity control and repeatability. Multi-cavity hot-runner molds on electric presses reduce flash and tuning time after mold changes.
