Party Building Unites Strength, Joint Party History Study Promotes Development
Date: 2021-06-30

Tsingshan Group and KIWL Group Party Organizations

Hold Party Building Exchange Activity

Stay True to Our Founding Mission   Keep Our Mission Firmly in Mind

On June 29, ahead of the Party's centenary, Tsingshan Group and KIWL Group held a joint Party Day activity to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the founding of the CPC and exchange experiences in Party building among private enterprises in the new era.

Led by KIWL Party Branch Secretary and Chairman Jiang Wei and Tsingshan Party Branch Secretary Sun Yuanlin, all Party members reviewed the oath of joining the Party and reaffirmed their original aspirations.

Party members visited KIWL's Party History School, Technology Experience Hall, robotic automatic lines, FMS flexible manufacturing line, intelligent control platform, and Class 100,000 clean assembly workshop to experience the driving force of innovation under Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era. At the employee cultural center, they exchanged views on building a harmonious home and employee wellbeing.

At the symposium, Secretary Sun Yuanlin said the exchange strengthened cultural identity and Party building innovation between the two sides, and expressed hope for deeper cooperation. Chairman Jiang Wei noted that Tsingshan Group is a global leader in stainless steel and new energy, and an benchmark KIWL has long admired. Under the guidance of Party history spirit, both sides will continue to strengthen exchanges and explore distinctive models of Party building in private enterprises.
