Date: 2021-12-01

Stay Firm in Direction and Forge Ahead on the New Era Journey

On November 30, the KIWL Group Party Branch held a special study session on "Studying and Implementing the Spirit of the Sixth Plenary Session of the 19th CPC Central Committee." Joint Party Branch Secretary and Chairman Jiang Wei led the study. All Party members, cadres, probationary members, and activists attended. Branch venues in Shanghai and Xuzhou joined synchronously.

The meeting pointed out

The Sixth Plenary Session of the 19th CPC Central Committee was convened at a major historical moment as the Party marked its centenary, achieved the first centenary goal of building a moderately prosperous society, and embarked on the second centenary goal of building a modern socialist country.

The meeting emphasized

Studying, publicizing, and implementing the spirit of the Sixth Plenary Session is a major political task for the Group at present and for the foreseeable future. All Party members and cadres must study the spirit thoroughly, grasp its essence, unify thought and action with the Session's requirements, and translate new ideas and measures into practical plans to promote high-quality development and forge ahead on the new era journey.

The meeting stressed

First, raise political awareness. All Party members should treat studying and implementing the Session's spirit as a top priority, study diligently, and ensure accurate understanding. Leading cadres should set an example and drive company-wide enthusiasm for implementation.

Second, plan carefully. All departments should work together under the Party Branch leadership. Department heads and assigned Party members are primarily responsible for ensuring full coverage of study and publicity.

Third, strengthen supervision. The Party Branch will include implementation of the Session's spirit in its oversight scope, track progress across units, and address new issues promptly.
