Specialized for medical equipment series

The S series high-speed precision for medical device is a specialized series developed by KIWL for manufacturing consumable products in the medical device industry. It has been designed to meet market requirements and reduce costs for customers through in-depth communication.
  • Specification

▶ With the central locking mold design concept, combined with the ultra-wide and large four-column inner spacing, as well as the extended mold opening stroke, the S series machine effectively reduces mold deformation, protects the mold, and increases its service life. This design makes the S series particularly suitable for processing medical device products.


▶ The adoption of European-style sheet metal fully enclosed design not only enhances the aesthetics and cleanliness of the machine's appearance but also facilitates easy cleaning. This design meets the environmental requirements of medical device production workshops.


▶ S series is equipped with dedicated screws and power configurations for medical products, enabling high speed and high precision operations. This configuration meets the requirements of medical equipment injection molding processes.


▶ A specialized water cooling system is designed specifically for medical equipment molds, making it convenient for connecting and disconnecting the water circuits. The system is designed to be aesthetically pleasing and well-organized, reducing the risk of water leakage.


▶ S series is equipped with card-sleeve structure, and the hydraulic oil circuit is designed without welding, effectively reducing the possibility of oil leakage by 99.99% and ensuring cleanliness in medical product manufacturing and the workshop.


▶ The H-ECO infrared nano heating system is standardly equipped, featuring energy efficiency, fast heating, and excellent insulation performance. It significantly reduces the workshop temperature, leading to electricity savings for the company.
