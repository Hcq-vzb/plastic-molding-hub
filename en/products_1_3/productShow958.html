▶ A newly designed mold locking structure. The moving template adopts a box-type four-point support and center-pressing mold locking design, effectively controlling the deformation of the template and mold, and adapting to the high-speed, high-pressure operation conditions for 24 hours without interruption.

▶ Modular combination design allows for the free selection of power units, injection units, and various specialized screws, enabling high-pressure, high-speed, high-precision performance to meet the requirements of various injection molding processes. 

▶ Utilizes a fully enclosed European-style sheet metal design, featuring an aesthetically pleasing and clean appearance that is easy to clean. It also offers customized appearance services to meet customer preferences.

▶ Equipped with a card sleeve structure as a standard feature across the entire series, effectively reducing the risk of oil leakage.
