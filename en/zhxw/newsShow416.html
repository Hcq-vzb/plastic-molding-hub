GRAND OPENING | Quan Tang Group SHINES AT CHINAPLAS 2025 INTERNATIONAL RUBBER & PLASTICS EXHIBITION
Time：2025-04-15

ON APRIL 15, 2025, THE SHENZHEN WORLD EXHIBITION & CONVENTION CENTER WILL HOST THE CHINAPLAS INTERNATIONAL PLASTICS AND RUBBER EXHIBITION. SINCE ITS INCEPTION IN 1983, IT HAS GROWN INTO ONE OF THE MOST INFLUENTIAL EXHIBITION IN THE PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY.


    

    


AS THE TRANSFORMATION OF GLOBAL MANUFACTURING CONTINUES TO ADVANCE, THE PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY IS UNDERGOING EVEN MORE PROFOUND INNOVATION. AT THIS YEAR'S CHINAPLAS INTERNATIONAL PLASTICS & RUBBER EXHIBITION, KIWL WILL SHOWCASE ITS NEWLY DEVELOPED SK140II HYBRID INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE AND SE180 ALL-ELECTRIC INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE AT BOOTH 12S31. AT THIS PREMIER INDUSTRY EXHIBITION REPRESENTING THE HIGHEST PROFESSIONAL STANDARDS AND TECHNOLOGIES OF PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY, KIWL WILL CONTINUE WIDENING THE BOUNDARIES OF PRECISION INJECTION MOLDING APPLICATIONS THROUGH ITS PROPRIETARY R&D TECHNOLOGIES.

AT THE EXHIBITION SITE, WAVE AFTER WAVE OF CUSTOMERS FROM ALL OVER THE WORLD ARE ATTRACTED BY THE UNIQUE CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE STRUCTURE DESIGN AND OUTSTANDING TECHNICAL ADVANTAGES OF KIWL MACHINES. CUSTOMERS COME AND HAVE A DEEPER BUSINESS NEGOTIATION WITH KIWL TEAM.
