Quan Tang Group ATTENDED THE 19TH EGYPT PLASTICS & RUBBER EXHIBITION (PLASTEX)
Time：2024-01-12

THE 19TH EGYPT PLASTICS & RUBBER EXHIBITION (PLASTEX), SUPPORTED BY THE EGYPTIAN MINISTRY OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY, OPENED ON JANUARY 9, 2024, LOCAL TIME, AT THE EGYPT INTERNATIONAL EXHIBITION CENTER FOR A FOUR-DAY RUN.

KIWL SHOWCASED ITS STAR PRODUCT, THE SK SERIES INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES, AT THIS EXHIBITION. THE COMPANY WILL BRING INNOVATIVE PRODUCTS AND A RICHER TECHNICAL EXPERIENCE TO CUSTOMERS, LOOKING FORWARD TO SHARING A TECHNOLOGICAL FEAST WITH OVERSEAS CLIENTS.


    

    


THIS EDITION FEATURED OVER 500 COMPANIES FROM MORE THAN 50 COUNTRIES, COVERING AN EXHIBITION AREA OF 20,000 SQUARE METERS. AS THE LARGEST EXHIBITION OF ITS KIND IN THE MIDDLE EAST AND NORTH AFRICA (MENA) REGION, PLASTEX IS DEDICATED TO SHOWCASING COMPREHENSIVE AND SUSTAINABLE PRODUCTS FOR THE MENA MARKET. THESE INCLUDE MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENT, RAW MATERIALS, SEMI-FINISHED PRODUCTS, PLASTIC PRODUCTS, PACKAGING SOLUTIONS, PARTS AND SPARES, ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND ROBOTICS, AUTOMATION, AND SMART SERVICES. IT IS POISED TO BECOME A VITAL PLATFORM FOR THE REGION TO FOCUS ON THE FUTURE, DRIVE INDUSTRIAL INNOVATION, AND ACHIEVE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT.

MEANWHILE, KIWL AIMS TO SEIZE MARKET OPPORTUNITIES THROUGH THIS EXHIBITION, STRENGTHEN COMMUNICATION WITH LOCAL DISTRIBUTORS, FURTHER EXPLORE THE EGYPTIAN MARKET, ENHANCE VALUE CREATION CAPABILITIES AND BRAND INFLUENCE, AND ACTIVELY EXPLORE MORE DEVELOPMENT PATHS. IN THE FUTURE, THE COMPANY WILL CONTINUE TO INCREASE R&D INVESTMENT, CONTINUOUSLY IMPROVE ITS PROCESS TECHNOLOGY AND R&D LEVEL, ADVANCE ITS LEAN PRODUCTION MODEL, OPTIMIZE PRODUCT STRUCTURE, AND PROVIDE HIGH-PERFORMANCE, HIGH-EFFICIENCY INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES FOR THE LOCAL MARKET, CONTRIBUTING TO THE WIN-WIN DEVELOPMENT OF THE REGIONAL ECONOMY.
