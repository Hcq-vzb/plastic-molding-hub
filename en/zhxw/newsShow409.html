Quan Tang Group ATTENDED THE 14TH CHINA (ZHENGZHOU) PLASTICS INDUSTRY EXPO
Time：2024-03-27

ON MARCH 27, 2024, THE 14TH CHINA (ZHENGZHOU) PLASTICS INDUSTRY EXPO OPENED AT THE ZHENGZHOU INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION AND EXHIBITION CENTER. LASTING FOR THREE DAYS WITH THE THEME "LOW CARBON, RECYCLING, INTELLIGENT MANUFACTURING," THE EXPO ATTRACTED OVER 600 UPSTREAM AND DOWNSTREAM ENTERPRISES FROM ACROSS THE PLASTICS INDUSTRY NATIONWIDE. THEY COLLECTIVELY SHOWCASED NEW PRODUCTS, TECHNOLOGIES, EQUIPMENT, AND ACHIEVEMENTS, COMPREHENSIVELY EMPOWERING THE INNOVATIVE DEVELOPMENT OF THE PLASTICS INDUSTRY.

KIWL PRESENTED ITS SK SERIES OIL-ELECTRIC HYBRID MACHINES AT THIS EXPO, ATTRACTING SIGNIFICANT ATTENTION FROM NUMEROUS VISITORS.。

    

    


ZHENGZHOU, AS AN IMPORTANT CITY IN CENTRAL CHINA, IS DEMONSTRATING STRONG DEVELOPMENT VITALITY IN VARIOUS FIELDS SUCH AS ECONOMY, TECHNOLOGY, AND CULTURE WITH ITS UNIQUE ADVANTAGES AND CHARM. TAKING THIS EXPO AS AN OPPORTUNITY, KIWL NOT ONLY SHOWCASED ITS STRENGTH AND INNOVATION CAPABILITIES BUT ALSO ENGAGED IN IN-DEPTH EXCHANGES AND COOPERATION DISCUSSIONS WITH INDUSTRY PROFESSIONALS FROM VARIOUS REGIONS. THIS HELPS ENHANCE THE VISIBILITY AND INFLUENCE OF KIWL INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES AND OPENS UP NEW BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES FOR THE COMPANY'S FUTURE DEVELOPMENT.

IN THE FUTURE, KIWL WILL CONTINUE TO MAKE GREATER BREAKTHROUGHS IN TECHNOLOGICAL INNOVATION, ENVIRONMENTALLY SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT, AND APPLICATION FIELD EXPANSION. IT REMAINS COMMITTED TO PROVIDING CUSTOMERS WITH HIGH-QUALITY PRODUCTS AND EXCELLENT SERVICES, PROMOTING THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE INJECTION MOLDING INDUSTRY, AND MAKING GREATER CONTRIBUTIONS TO SOCIAL PROGRESS.
