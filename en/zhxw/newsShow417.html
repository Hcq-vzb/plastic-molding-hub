KIWL IN 2025 SAUDI PLASTICS ＆ PETROCHEM EXHIBITION
Time：2025-05-16

THE 2025 SAUDI PLASTICS＆PETROCHEM EXHIBITION GRAND OPENING AT THE RIYADH INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION AND EXHIBITION CENTER OVER FOUR DAYS FROM MAY 12 TO 15, 2025.

THE SAUDI PLASTICS＆PETROCHEM EXHIBITION AS ONE OF THE MOST IMPORTANT INDUSTRIAL EVENTS IN THE PLASTIC, RUBBER AND PETROCHEMICAL IN MIDDLE EAST, HOLDING A PIVOTAL POSITION BOTH WITHIN THE REGION AND GLOBALLY. IN RECENT YEARS, THE SAUDI MARKET HAS EXPERIENCED RAPID GROWTH, ATTRACTING INVESTMENTS FROM ENTERPRISES WORLDWIDE AND DEMONSTRATING IMMENSE DEVELOPMENT POTENTIAL.


    

    


THE KIWL TEAM AND ITS LOCAL AGENT TEAM SHOWS THE NEWLY DEVELOPED SK140 CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE DESIGN INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE AT BOOTH 1-215 DURING THE EXHIBITION. 

KIWL MACHINES STOOD OUT AMONG COMPETITORS WITH ITS UNIQUE CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE STRUCTURE, OFFERING DISTINCT ADVANTAGES OF "LARGE CAPACITY, ENERGY EFFICIENCY, AND OPERATIONAL STABILITY". THIS INNOVATION ATTRACTED NUMEROUS MIDDLE EASTERN CLIENTS AND INVESTORS FOR IN-DEPTH DISCUSSIONS, WITH MANY EXPRESSING STRONG APPRECIATION FOR THE UNIQUE DESIGN OF KIWL’S CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE DESIGN.

THROUGH THIS EXHIBITION, KIWL NOT ONLY FULLY SHOWCASED ITS ROBUST TECHNOLOGICAL CAPABILITIES, BUT ALSO DEEPENED THE IMPLEMENTATION OF ITS LOCALIZED STRATEGIC INITIATIVES BY ACTIVELY ALIGNING WITH SAUDI ARABIA'S "VISION 2030". THIS INITIATIVE HAS LAID A MORE SOLID FOUNDATION FOR KIWL'S SUBSEQUENT DEVELOPMENT IN THE MIDDLE EAST REGION, FURTHER IMPLEMENTED THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT PHILOSOPHY, AND ACCELERATED THE COMPANY'S STRATEGIC LAYOUT PACE IN COUNTRIES ALONG THE BELT AND ROAD INITIATIVE.
