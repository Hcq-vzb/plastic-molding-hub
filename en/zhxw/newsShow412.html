Quan Tang Group SHINES AT IRAN PLAST 2024 INTERNATIONAL EXHIBITION
Time：2024-09-12

ON SEPTEMBER 8TH LOCAL TIME, THE HIGHLY ANTICIPATED 18TH IRAN INTERNATIONAL PLASTICS & RUBBER EXHIBITION (IRAN PLAST 2024) GRANDLY OPENED AT THE TEHRAN INTERNATIONAL PERMANENT FAIRGROUND. LASTING FOR FOUR DAYS (SEPTEMBER 8-11, 2024), THE EXHIBITION GATHERED ELITE ENTERPRISES FROM THE GLOBAL PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY TO SHOWCASE THE LATEST TECHNOLOGICAL ACHIEVEMENTS AND INDUSTRY TRENDS.    

    

    

    


AT THIS EXHIBITION, KIWL SHOWCASED ITS LATEST MODELS: THE SK140 CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE, THE SK350 CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE INJECTION MOLDING MACHINE, AND THE SK300-PET PREFORM SPECIAL MACHINE. LEVERAGING KIWL'S UNIQUE CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE STRUCTURE AND INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGY WITH A HIGH-QUALITY SERVICE CONCEPT, KIWL MADE A SIGNIFICANT MARK AT THIS IRAN PLAST EXHIBITION. IN THE FUTURE, KIWL WILL CONTINUE TO UPHOLD THE PHILOSOPHY OF "QUALITY FIRST, INNOVATION LEADS," CONTRIBUTING MORE POWER TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE GLOBAL PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY

THE MIDDLE EAST MARKET, AS ONE OF THE REGIONS WITH THE FASTEST-GROWING DEMAND FOR PLASTIC AND RUBBER PRODUCTS GLOBALLY, HOLDS ENORMOUS MARKET POTENTIAL. KIWL INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES HAS ALWAYS REGARDED THE MIDDLE EAST AS A CRUCIAL STRATEGIC MARKET. THE IRAN PLAST EXHIBITION PROVIDED A VALUABLE OPPORTUNITY FOR US TO FURTHER EXPAND OUR MIDDLE EAST BUSINESS. THROUGH THIS EXHIBITION, WE COULD NOT ONLY DEEPEN OUR UNDERSTANDING OF THE LOCAL MARKET BUT ALSO ESTABLISH SOLID COOPERATIVE RELATIONSHIPS WITH CLIENTS IN IRAN AND SURROUNDING COUNTRIES.

IN THE FUTURE, KIWL INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES WILL CONTINUE TO INCREASE INVESTMENT IN THE MIDDLE EAST MARKET, CONTINUOUSLY OPTIMIZE PRODUCTS AND SERVICES, AND MEET THE DIVERSE NEEDS OF CUSTOMERS. WE BELIEVE THAT THROUGH THE SUCCESSFUL PARTICIPATION IN IRAN PLAST, KIWL WILL CREATE VALUE FOR MORE CUSTOMERS AND JOINTLY PROMOTE THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF THE PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY.
