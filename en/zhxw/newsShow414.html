EAR-END GRAND FINALE EXHIBITION | Quan Tang Group SHINES AT PLAST EURASIA ISTANBUL 2024
Time：2024-12-09

FROM DECEMBER 4TH TO 7TH, 2024, LOCAL TIME, THE 34TH INTERNATIONAL ISTANBUL PLASTIC INDUSTRY FAIR (PLAST EURASIA ISTANBUL) GRANDLY OPENED AT THE ISTANBUL TUYAP EXHIBITION AND CONGRESS CENTER. KIWL, TOGETHER WITH ITS LOCAL AGENT, MADE A SPLENDID APPEARANCE! AS A PRACTITIONER IN THE FIELD OF PLASTIC MACHINERY, KIWL INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES MADE A STUNNING DEBUT WITH CUTTING-EDGE TECHNOLOGY AND INNOVATIVE ACHIEVEMENTS, ATTRACTING NUMEROUS PROFESSIONAL GAZES FROM AROUND THE GLOBE. ITS SK SERIES INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES BECAME THE FOCUS WITH THEIR EXQUISITE DESIGN AND OUTSTANDING PERFORMANCE.        


    

    


AT THE EXHIBITION SITE, KIWL'S PROFESSIONAL TEAM PATIENTLY ANSWERED QUESTIONS, DEMONSTRATING THE HIGH SPEED, EFFICIENCY, AND PRECISION ADVANTAGES OF THE INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES THROUGH IMPRESSIVE DEMONSTRATIONS. CLIENTS FROM EUROPE, ASIA, AND THE MIDDLE EAST EXPRESSED COOPERATION INTENTIONS, RECOGNIZING KIWL'S TECHNICAL STRENGTH, R&D POTENTIAL, AND COMMITMENT TO QUALITY, VIEWING IT AS A RELIABLE PARTNER FOR EXPANSION AND UPGRADING IN THE PLASTIC PROCESSING FIELD. TURKEY, WITH ITS EXCEPTIONAL GEOGRAPHICAL ADVANTAGE SPANNING ASIA AND EUROPE, RESEMBLES A SOLID BRIDGE CLOSELY CONNECTING EASTERN AND WESTERN MARKETS, OPENING UP VAST AVENUES FOR INTERNATIONAL TRADE IN THE PLASTICS INDUSTRY.

AT THIS EXHIBITION, KIWL NOT ONLY SHOWCASED ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY AND HIGH-QUALITY PRODUCTS BUT ALSO DEMONSTRATED ITS HIGH REGARD FOR AND DETERMINATION TO DEEPLY CULTIVATE THE INTERNATIONAL MARKET. WITH INNOVATION AS ITS WINGS AND QUALITY AS ITS VESSEL, IT SET SAIL AT THE TURKISH RUBBER & PLASTICS EXHIBITION TOWARDS THE BROADER OCEAN OF THE OVERSEAS PLASTIC MACHINERY INDUSTRY, INJECTING STRONG MOMENTUM INTO INDUSTRY DEVELOPMENT AND JOINTLY CREATING A BRILLIANT FUTURE WITH OVERSEAS PARTNERS.
