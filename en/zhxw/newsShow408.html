Quan Tang Group ATTENDED THE 31ST CHINA (WENZHOU) INTERNATIONAL INDUSTRY EXPO
Time：2024-03-11

THE 31ST CHINA (WENZHOU) INTERNATIONAL INDUSTRY EXPO WAS HELD AT THE WENZHOU CONVENTION AND EXHIBITION CENTER FROM MARCH 8-10, 2024. KIWL PARTICIPATED, SHOWCASING ITS NEWLY UPGRADED SK SERIES INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES


AS ONE OF THE EARLIEST THREE EXHIBITIONS IN WENZHOU TO RECEIVE UFI INTERNATIONAL CERTIFICATION, THE WENZHOU INDUSTRY EXPO, BASED IN WENZHOU, RADIATES TO MANUFACTURING CLUSTERS IN SOUTHERN ZHEJIANG, NORTHERN FUJIAN, EASTERN JIANGXI, AND THE YANGTZE RIVER DELTA REGION. WITH DECADES OF INDUSTRY EXPERTISE, IT IS HAILED AS THE "ANNUAL BELLWETHER OF MANUFACTURING IN SOUTHERN ZHEJIANG AND NORTHERN FUJIAN."

  

  


AS A COMPANY DEEPLY ROOTED IN WENZHOU, KIWL UNDERSTANDS ITS RESPONSIBILITY AND MISSION. IT HAS AN IN-DEPTH UNDERSTANDING OF LOCAL CONSUMER NEEDS, ENABLING IT TO PROVIDE MORE CONSIDERATE SERVICES AND PRODUCTS TO CUSTOMERS WHILE ALSO ACCURATELY GRASPING MARKET DYNAMICS AND PRECISELY ADJUSTING BUSINESS STRATEGIES. KIWL'S PRODUCTS AND SERVICES ARE NOT MERELY TRANSACTION MEDIUMS; THEY ARE BRIDGES CONNECTING US WITH CUSTOMERS, CONVEYING RESPECT FOR LOCAL CONSUMPTION CULTURE AND COMMITMENT TO SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT.

IN THE FUTURE, KIWL WILL CONTINUE TO UPHOLD THE BUSINESS PHILOSOPHY OF "INNOVATION KNOWS NO BOUNDARIES, PURSUING SUSTAINABLE OPERATION," STAY TRUE TO ITS ORIGINAL ASPIRATIONS, DEEPEN ITS PRESENCE IN THE LOCAL MARKET, GROW TOGETHER WITH THE INDUSTRY, AND CREATE A BETTER FUTURE.
