KIWL IN PLASTEX 2026
Time：2026-01-15

FROM JANUARY 9 TO JANUARY 12, 2026, PLASTEX CAIRO, ONE OF THE LARGEST PLASTIC EXHIBITIONS IN THE AFRICAN REGION, GRANDLY OPENED AT THE EGYPT INTERNATIONAL EXHIBITION CENTER IN CAIRO, EGYPT.

THIS EXHIBITION ATTRACTED NOT ONLY NUMEROUS ENTERPRISES FROM AROUND THE WORLD IN THE PLASTIC MACHINERY AND RELATED INDUSTRIES BUT ALSO THE ATTENTION OF MANY INDIVIDUALS AND COMPANIES SEEKING TO DEVELOP AND GROW IN THE EGYPTIAN AND AFRICAN MARKETS.

    

    

AT THIS EXHIBITION, KIWL, TOGETHER WITH ITS LOCAL AGENCY TEAM, DISPLAYED THE LATEST MODELS OF THE SK140 AND SK700 CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE INJECTION MOLDING MACHINES, SPECIFICALLY DESIGNED FOR THE CHARACTERISTICS OF THE EGYPTIAN MARKET. LEVERAGING KIWL'S UNIQUE CENTRAL LOCKING TOGGLE STRUCTURE, INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGY, AND HIGH-QUALITY SERVICE PHILOSOPHY, THE DISPLAY DREW SIGNIFICANT ATTENTION FROM INDUSTRY PEERS AND VISITORS AT THE EGYPT PLASTEX CAIRO EXHIBITION AND EARNED HIGH PRAISE WITHIN THE INDUSTRY.

FOR THE EGYPTIAN MARKET, THE KIWL FRUIT FRAME SPECIAL MACHINE HAS THE FOLLOWING FEATURES:

▼OVERALL MACHINE FRAME IS HEIGHTENED AND REINFORCED FOR ENHANCED RIGIDITY, FACILITATING MANUAL HANDLING;

▼FULLY UPGRADED PLASTICIZING MECHANISM WITH DOUBLED WEAR RESISTANCE;

▼HIGH-RESPONSIVENESS SERVO SYSTEM FOR MORE POWERFUL PERFORMANCE;

▼NEW LARGE-SCREEN CONTROL SYSTEM FOR MORE PRECISE CONTROL AND USER-FRIENDLY INTERACTION.


THE EGYPTIAN MARKET, AS ONE OF THE FASTEST-GROWING AND MOST DEMANDING PLASTIC MARKETS IN THE AFRICAN REGION, HOLDS IMMENSE MARKET POTENTIAL. KIWL HAS ALWAYS REGARDED THE EGYPTIAN MARKET AS A KEY STRATEGIC MARKET AND HAS GROWN AND ADVANCED TOGETHER WITH THE MARKET IN COLLABORATION WITH ITS LOCAL AGENCY TEAM. THROUGH THIS EXHIBITION, KIWL NOT ONLY DEEPENED ITS UNDERSTANDING OF THE LOCAL MARKET BUT ALSO ESTABLISHED SOLID COOPERATIVE RELATIONSHIPS WITH CLIENTS FROM EGYPT AND NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES.

LOOKING AHEAD, KIWL WILL FURTHER INCREASE ITS INVESTMENT IN THE EGYPTIAN MARKET AND THE BROADER AFRICAN MARKET, CONTINUOUSLY OPTIMIZING PRODUCTS AND SERVICES TO MEET THE DIVERSE NEEDS OF CUSTOMERS. WE BELIEVE THAT THROUGH THE SUCCESSFUL PARTICIPATION IN THE PLASTEX CAIRO EXHIBITION, KIWL WILL CREATE VALUE FOR MORE CUSTOMERS AND JOINTLY PROMOTE THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF THE PLASTICS AND RUBBER INDUSTRY.
