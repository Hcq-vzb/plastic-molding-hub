Yuan Xiao Festival: Happiness resides in KIWL
Time：2022-02-15


To ensure that all KIWL family members have a happy and joyful Lantern Festival, on February 15th, KIWL Group held a "Welcoming the Year of Renyin, Gathering in the Full Moon" carnival. Everyone gathered to guess lantern riddles, play games, and win prizes, igniting the passion of all employees and working together towards common prosperity.


Entering the game area, some people enthusiastically participated in the "Happiness Ring Toss," while others enjoyed the "Rolling for Wealth" game, and some favored the "Flying towards a New Future" activity. The atmosphere of the event was lively and spirited, with participants playing and laughing, returning home with a sense of fulfillment.


Through these entertaining games, the KIWL family shared the joy and happiness of the festival. Senior employees dispelled their homesickness and brought forth the passion for progress, while new colleagues experienced the warmth of the "family culture" and quickly integrated into the united big family.


The Lantern Festival event came to a close amidst the joyous and bustling atmosphere, leaving a perfect ending for this lively festival. It allowed all KIWL employees to enjoy the fruits of the company's development and strengthen their sense of mission as members of KIWL.


KIWL employees are dedicated and passionate in their work, and their lives are filled with happiness and contentment. The "Welcoming the Year of Renyin, Gathering in the Full Moon" event brought joy to the KIWL family, fostering happiness in both body and mind. It also empowers us to embark on the journey ahead with firmness and enthusiasm. In the new year, we will sail with this strength and passion, overcoming challenges and forging ahead to create new glory. Together, we will stride confidently towards a prosperous future on the path that belongs to KIWL.


Finally, KIWL Group wishes all KIWL family members a happy Lantern Festival!
