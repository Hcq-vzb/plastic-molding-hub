The "Goddess Red" event：Highlighting the "Power of Her"
Time：2023-03-08


They, never forget their initial intentions, shoulder responsibilities with tenderness. They, adhere to their mission, writing the most beautiful chapter of their lives. They are the women of the new era, embodying the spiritual connotation of KIWL's "Goodness is Holiness, Hard Work Leads to a Prosperous Nation."


In the bright spring of March, KIWL Group held a "Goddess Red" event on International Women's Day to showcase the strength of women for the female employees of the company.

Colorful and beautiful roses were the company's best wishes to the goddesses. Activities such as DIY fans, power transmitting hula hoops, and luck balloons showcased the diverse talents and feminine charm of the KIWL goddesses.


With their hearts devoted to the Party, they embark on a new journey. We pay tribute to the dedicated and courageous KIWL women, wishing you to shine both in light makeup and strong strategies!


Today is the 113th International Women's Day. Happy Women's Day to every woman!
