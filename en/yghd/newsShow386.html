"Grateful for Family, Creating the Future": Family Appreciation Day Theme Event
Time：2022-08-01

To enhance employees' sense of achievement, happiness, and belonging, and strengthen the connection between the company and employees' families, KIWL Group Limited held the second edition of the "Grateful for Family, Creating the Future" Family Appreciation Day theme event on July 31st.


Employees and their families had the opportunity to visit various facilities within the company, including the KIWL Party History School, KIWL Technology Experience Hall, Intelligent Workshop, Sports Stadium, and the Employee Living Area. By immersing themselves in their loved ones' work environment, family members gained a firsthand understanding of their work responsibilities and shared in the company's high-quality development achievements.


The joyful atmosphere of the games in the living area promoted interaction between parents and children, bringing employees closer to the company.


Finally, employees and their families gathered at the self-service restaurant in KIWL Group, where they enjoyed a delicious and abundant meal. This created a sense of happiness and harmony, allowing employees and their families to experience the joy of coming together.
