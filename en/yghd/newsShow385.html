Yuan Xiao Festival: A New Beginning, Spring Returns to the Earth
Time：2023-02-05


The Yuan Xiao are rounded in a circle, and the spring lantern routing in a circle. We celebrate Yuan Xiao Festival for the season circle begins. In order to promote traditional culture and celebrate the joyful festival of reunion, KIWL Group held the "Joyful Lantern Riddles, Happy Yuan Xiao Festival" fun activity, adding vibrant colors to the Yuan Xiao Festival and allowing everyone to enjoy a warm and pleasant festival, together experiencing the charm of traditional culture.


This Yuan Xiao Festival riddle activity created a warm, joyful, harmonious, and lively festive atmosphere for the employees, receiving unanimous praise from the staff.


Through this activity, not only did the employees gain an understanding of the cultural significance of the Yuan Xiao Festival, but it also deepened their awareness of inheriting and promoting traditional customs. It also enlivened the company's familial culture, strengthened communication among employees, enhanced cohesion, and stimulated employees' enthusiasm, playing a positive role in building a harmonious company.
