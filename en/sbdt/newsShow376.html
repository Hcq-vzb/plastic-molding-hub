Building a medical enterprise alliance to create a harmonious community together
Time：2022-04-14


On April 13th, KIWL Group Co., Ltd. and Longwan District First People's Hospital signed a cooperation framework agreement to initiate the medical enterprise party building alliance, emphasizing party building and promoting development, collaborative cooperation, and mutual assistance.


Zhang Wanli, the Secretary of the Party Committee of Longwan District First People's Hospital, and Shao Mindi, the Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee and the President, along with their delegation, visited KIWL Group Co., Ltd., where they toured the Party History School, Technology Department, Intelligent Production Line, and Activity Center. The signing and unveiling ceremony took place at the Zunyi Meeting Room in the Party History School of KIWL Group. Secretary Zhang Wanli and Secretary Jiang Wei jointly unveiled the "Medical Enterprise Party Building Alliance," while President Shao Mindi and General Manager Jiang Hong signed the medical enterprise cooperation framework agreement and unveiled the "Healthy, Happy, and Harmonious" community.


Secretary Zhang Wanli of Longwan District First People's Hospital expressed deep impressions after visiting the Party History School of KIWL Group. As a private enterprise, KIWL's emphasis and investment in party building for non-public enterprises, as well as their dedication to corporate culture development, are commendable. He hopes that this event will serve as a starting point for both parties to rely on party building leadership, strengthen business exchanges, enhance healthcare benefits, and build a harmonious community.

Jiang Wei, the Party Branch Secretary and Chairman of KIWL Group Co., Ltd., stated that "harmony" is an essential part of KIWL's corporate culture. KIWL Group and Longwan District First People's Hospital are located in the same area and share deep roots. The formation of the party building alliance between KIWL Group and Longwan District First People's Hospital establishes a partnership and follows the trend of the times, working together to forge a path of shared prosperity and mutual success.


Looking ahead, both parties will take the party building alliance as an opportunity to complement each other's strengths and weaknesses, explore new ideas for medical enterprise cooperation, and open a new chapter in medical enterprise collaboration. They will contribute to the development of Longwan District and contribute to the goal of "continuing the history of innovation and embarking on a path of shared prosperity" in Wenzhou.
