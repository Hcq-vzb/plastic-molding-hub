Academic-Industry Cooperation: Moving Forward Together
Time：2022-03-18


On March 17th, KIWL Group reached a strategic cooperation agreement with Wenzhou Polytechnic, aiming to achieve complementary advantages, resource sharing, mutual benefits, and common development.


Qian Qiang, Secretary of the Party Committee of Wenzhou Polytechnic, Zhou Wenlong, President and Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee, Guo Jing, Secretary of the Discipline Inspection Commission, Wu Xiangpeng, Hu Xingen, and Sun Furong, Vice Presidents, Jin Qingliang, Party Committee Member and Minister of the Organization Department, and other leaders visited KIWL Group. They visited the Party History School, Technology Hall, Intelligent Production Line, and Activity Center. The signing and unveiling ceremony took place in the Zunyi Meeting Room of the KIWL Group's Party History School. Secretary Qian Qiang and Secretary Jiang Wei jointly unveiled the "Academic-Industry Party Building Community." Vice President Hu Xingen and General Manager Jiang Hong signed the strategic cooperation agreement between the university and the enterprise. Vice President Sun Furong and General Manager Jiang Hong jointly unveiled the "Industry-University-Research Base."


During the discussion, both parties introduced their development processes, party building work, and future plans, and conducted in-depth discussions on strategic cooperation in talent cultivation, industry-education integration, scientific research and development, and party building.


Secretary Qian Qiang of Wenzhou Polytechnic expressed that KIWL Group, deeply rooted in Wenzhou and dedicated to the field of hydraulics, has achieved leapfrog development in terms of "striving for entrepreneurship, innovation and intelligence, party building guidance, and cultural strength." Wenzhou Polytechnic actively responds to the call of the Municipal Party Committee and Government, insists on universities entering enterprises, promotes university-industry cooperation, and deepens the integration of industry and education. The cooperation with KIWL Group provides practical innovation for teaching and research in the school and realizes the virtuous development of "industry, academia, and research" and the multiple win-win situations of "university, enterprise, and students." The establishment of the Party Building Community with KIWL will help explore new ideas for grassroots party building work and form a new pattern of grassroots party building work.


Jiang Wei, Chairman of KIWL Group, stated that KIWL highly values high-quality professional and technical talents, and by providing a platform for talent to display their abilities, they aim to achieve a future of mutual creation. The comprehensive and in-depth cooperation and exchange with Wenzhou Polytechnic will combine production and operation with scientific research and practice, complementing each other's strengths and advantages. It is hoped that through KIWL's "red gene" and local elements, outstanding science and engineering students will be attracted to join KIWL, enhancing communication and collaboration between the university and the enterprise, leveraging the technological innovation advantages of universities, and meeting the high-quality development needs of the enterprise.


Looking ahead, both parties sincerely hope that this university-industry cooperation will enable them to join hands and leverage their respective advantages. They aim to achieve mutual benefit and win-win results, and contribute to regional development through reciprocal exchanges and the realization of dreams in the Oujiang area.
