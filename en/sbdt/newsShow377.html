Wenzhou Mayor Yao visit KIWL Group to inspect the energy consumption "dual control" work
Time：2021-09-02

On the afternoon of September 1st, Deputy Secretary of the Wenzhou Municipal Party Committee and Mayor Yao Gaoyuan, along with their delegation, visited KIWL Group Co., Ltd. for a special research on the implementation of energy consumption "dual control" and the transformational development of the company. They were accompanied by Secretary-General Chen Jun of the Municipal Government, Longwan District Governor Xia Yujiao, Director Huang Yangxu of the Municipal Development and Reform Commission, and Director Jia Huanxiang of the Municipal Economic and Information Bureau.


General Manager Jiang Hong reported on the progress of KIWL's energy-saving and technological transformation work and the company's recent business development. Mayor Yao pointed out that implementing the "dual control" action on energy consumption is an important means to promote ecological civilization construction and facilitate the transformation of enterprise development models. He expressed the hope that KIWL would continue to consolidate the achievements already made and fully implement the new development concept while carefully constructing a new development model.


Mayor Yao and his delegation visited KIWL's Party History School, Technology Experience Hall, and Intelligent Manufacturing Factory. He highly praised KIWL's achievements in party building, cultural development, and innovation and application of new technologies. He encouraged KIWL to continue making good use of synchronized manufacturing resources from abroad, promote the deep transformation of intelligent manufacturing and green manufacturing, and take the lead in green development, becoming a pioneer and leader in the process of building Wenzhou as a demonstration zone for common prosperity.
