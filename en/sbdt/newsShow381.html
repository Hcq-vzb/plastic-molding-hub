To empower intelligent manufacturing in enterprises and jointly create industrial prosperity
Time：2021-05-14

On May 13th, Deputy Mayor Zhang Ke of Xuzhou City, accompanied by Deputy Director Dai Lei of the Xuzhou Economic Development Zone Administrative Committee and other officials, visited and guided the work at KIWL Group. Chairman Jiang Wei and General Manager Jiang Hong, among other senior executives, accompanied them during the visit.


Deputy Mayor Zhang Ke and his delegation visited various facilities within KIWL, including the Party History School, Technology Experience Hall, intelligent production line, logistics control platform, 100,000-level dust-free assembly workshop, and injection molding workshop. They gained a detailed understanding of the development process of KIWL, its distinctive party-building efforts, and technological innovation. Deputy Mayor Zhang highly praised the company for its reliance on intelligent equipment development and innovative smart manufacturing models.


Chairman Jiang emphasized KIWL's dedication to the field of electro-hydraulic transmission over the past 28 years and provided insights into the company's product portfolio, market development, as well as the industrial layout, intelligent manufacturing projects, and plans for the industrial research institute in Xuzhou. He expressed confidence that with the support and care from the Xuzhou municipal government and the Economic Development Zone, KIWL's new industrial projects will be implemented and successful at an accelerated pace.


Deputy Mayor Zhang Ke expressed that his visit to KIWL Group allowed him to experience the strong sense of mission and dedication among the employees. The Xuzhou municipal government has identified engineering machinery and intelligent manufacturing as the top strategic directions for urban economic development, which aligns well with KIWL's pursuits and future vision. The delegation also witnessed the entrepreneurial spirit of private enterprises from Wenzhou. Deputy Mayor Zhang believes that, with the Xuzhou KIWL intelligent manufacturing project as a catalyst, both parties will strengthen collaboration and jointly contribute to the continuous development of the local economy and the flourishing of the industry.
