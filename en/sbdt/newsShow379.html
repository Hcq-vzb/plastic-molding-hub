KIWL Group awarded the title of Wenzhou "Most Beautiful Factory"
Time：2022-02-12

On February 10th, the Publicity Department of the Wenzhou Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Bureau of Economy and Information Technology jointly announced the list of "Most Beautiful Factories" in Wenzhou City for the year 2021, and KIWL Group successfully made it to the list.


Over the years, KIWL has been dedicated to the construction of a harmonious natural and cultural environment within the factory. The factory area features clean and spacious roads, clear functional divisions, a well-planned layout, beautiful surroundings, and comprehensive supporting facilities. KIWL takes it as its responsibility to inherit red culture and establish a century-old enterprise, continuously promoting party building and cultural development within the company. This has resulted in a unique ecosystem of private economic party building and a distinctive corporate culture characterized by innovation, craftsmanship, hard work, and harmony.


In the future, KIWL will live up to expectations, make persistent efforts, strive to become a practitioner of "Beautiful Wenzhou," a leader in the high-quality development of the manufacturing industry, and a pioneer in intelligent manufacturing. It will contribute to the creation of Wenzhou's advanced areas in the new era for "Two Healthy" initiatives and independent innovation demonstration zones.
