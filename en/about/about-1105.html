An Excellent Chinese Supplier Of Intelligent
Manufacturing Injection Molding Equipment

Shanghai Quantang Ecological Technology Group Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "KIWL") is a brand enterprise established in 2002. It is primarily engaged in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of automated intelligent equipment, intelligent integration systems, and rubber and plastic machinery. With the continuous efforts and innovative spirit of over 30 research and technical personnel, KIWL has successfully launched various professional injection molding machines, including machines for medical devices, shoe heels, cable ties, and pen production. In the future, KIWL will introduce the SK series of mechanical direct-pressure injection molding machines to the market. The SK series injection molding machines, with their unique advantages, will become the flagship products of KIWL, and the company has also begun to develop a series of two-plate machines.
