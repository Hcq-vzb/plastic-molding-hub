PET container

PET containers and PET preforms, which are thick-walled packaging products, are widely used in the daily cosmetics industry. They are characterized by their large weight, thick walls, high cleanliness, and strong design aesthetics. Saintbond offers professional PET material thick-walled molding solutions that ensure both production efficiency and the stability, precision, and cleanliness of plastic products.


Specifications：

● Utilization of PET-specific high-plasticization components with a length-to-diameter ratio of 1:24, enhancing plasticization speed and efficiency for high production efficiency.

● Strict control over mold closure injection and temperature control, ensuring high precision of plastic products.

● Flexible product combinations are available to achieve optimal resource allocation and improve cost-effectiveness of investments.

● We can provide customers with customized and well-matched comprehensive solutions that are easy to operate and offer high cost-effectiveness, maximizing benefits for our clients.
