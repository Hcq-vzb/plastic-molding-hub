Cycling container

Cycling container specialized injection molding machine is dedicated to the production of storage boxes, turnover crates, and large storage containers. It adopts electric pre-plasticization technology to save energy and meets the first-level energy consumption standards, with a maximum power of 365kW. Its advanced design concept meets the needs of users in different production scenarios, providing energy-saving solutions and creating greater cost advantages.


Specifications:

C-type high-plasticization and high-mixing screw, ensuring good color mixing effect.

Increased power of the oil pump motor by 1-2 levels for enhanced performance.
